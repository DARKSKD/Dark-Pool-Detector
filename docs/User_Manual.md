# User Manual – Dark Pool Detective Platform

## Starting Surveillance
Click **Start Surveillance** to begin live monitoring.

Trades are generated and analyzed in real time.

---

## Monitoring Tab
- View live trades
- Observe active alerts
- Severity-coded alerts:
  - HIGH (Red)
  - MEDIUM (Orange)
  - LOW (Green)

---

## Forensics Tab
- Select symbol
- View price movement
- Analyze VWAP, volume, and price impact
- Investigate suspicious behavior

---

## Analytics Tab
- Market-wide volume distribution
- Buy vs Sell statistics
- Symbol-level comparison

---

## Clearing Data
Use **Clear Data** to reset:
- Trades
- Alerts
- Analytics

---

## Intended Users
- Market surveillance teams
- Compliance analysts
- Quant researchers
